

Re-admission Case Management System
International Project
Under the EURA agreement between Government of Pakistan and EU Member States, NADRA developed an Electronic Platform for Readmission of persons residing in EU without authorization. First phase of pilot project was rolled out for 4x EU Member States (MS) in 2017/18. Upon successful execution in Phase-I, second service agreement was signed in July 2020 between NADRA and IOM Pakistan for deployment of solution in additional 14x EU Foreign Missions and its corresponding states. A prior approval was acquired from MoI, MoFA, Law Division, MoHR, MoOP & HRD and Honorable Cabinet to sign the contract and deploy the solution. In compliance to second agreement, the system is deployed in additional 4 EU MS during month of Sep. & Oct. 2021.

Civil Registration System Sudan
International Project
Civil registration system was designed for the registration and tracking of vital events of the citizens of Sudan. These vital events include birth, death, marriage and divorce updated in a Centralized National Database. NADRA has developed and implemented an end to end civil registration management application for Sudan including issuance of National Identities, Birth, Death, Marriage and Divorce. The system is integrated with world leading AFIS system.

National Driver’s License System Bangladesh
International Project
NADRA has a comprehensive Drivers Licensing system for Bangladesh. These cards have stored data with card bearer’s personal information. Digitization of the applicant’s information ensures authenticity with transparency. Over 275,000 driver’s license have been issued. Additionally, NADRA provides regular consultancy services and enhancements to Bangladeshi Licensing systems.

Passport Issuance & Control System Kenya
International Project
NADRA enabled Kenya with the issuance of machine readable passport after extensive research and development. This Passport is Identity Card centric and compliant to ICAO standards. The implemented technology and business logic has enabled Kenya with International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) complaint data acquisition software for passports. Election Management System Fiji
International Project
Fiji Elections Office (FEO) awarded the “Election Management System” to NADRA through International Competitive Bidding process in May 2016. NADRA shall develop and deploy an ISO compliant, strategically secure and highly scalable election management system for the Republic of Fiji by February 2017. NADRA’s knowledge and experience shall be applied to migrate Fiji’s existing system of distributed and disparate Election Management modules to an enhanced and integrated architecture of a Centralized Election Management System (CEMS). This system aims to provide better and faster decision making while protecting the fundamental voting rights of the Fijian people.

National Identification System Somalia
International Project
In 2017, the Government of Pakistan and the Federal Government of Somalia entered into a contract to develop an ID System for Somali citizens. As a part of this agreement, NADRA is collaborating with the Government of Somalia to build a state-of-the-art national data and registration system. NADRA’s involvement encompasses the development of technology, provision of software, and supply of equipment to facilitate the national identification system. This collaborative effort aims to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of identification processes for the citizens of Somalia.

Excise and Taxation Department Vehicle Registration Card
Local Project
Excise and Taxation Department has started to issue the Vehicle Registration Card by replacing the existing book with the chip-based smart card. The vehicle and owner information is provided in the Smart Card. The owner of the project is Excise & Taxation Department. After performing the data entry at ETO office, NADRA executes verification and validation checks on the provided data from ETO. Chip based card is then printed by NADRA when the data has been verified and validated. NADRA in collaboration with ICTA (Excise & Taxation Department) is providing Vehicle registration and Transfer facility for residents of Islamabad at their Doorstep. Sehat Sahulat Program (Federal & Punjab and KP)
Local Project
The program strives to provide a cash less health scheme for underprivileged (BISP beneficiaries having PMT score less than 32.5) in a phased manner. The project is operational in Federal, Punjab, KP & Tharparkar Sindh, for all permanent residents of these areas under which beneficiaries will be eligible to avail health care services without any financial obligation with their Computerized National Identity Number only.

Electronic Business Invitation Letter
Local Project
NADRA designed, developed, implemented and hosts E-Business Invitation Letter System (E-BIL). E-BIL is developed to ensure that Foreign Nationals can apply for Business Invitation Letter (a REQUIRED document in Pakistan’s Business Visa Application) from their desired Chambers of Commerce and Industries / Trade Bodies authorized by the DGTO, Ministry of Commerce. E-BIL was launched in March 2020. Through E-BIL, Pakistani Nationals are able to apply for Business Invitation Letter as well to pursue business visa application for foreign countries.

Power of Attorney
Local Project
NADRA in collaboration with MoFA is tasked to automate the process of Power of Attorney (PoA) for Overseas Pakistanis. NADRA created an automated solution that allows Overseas Pakistanis to apply for PoA issuance from the comfort of their homes without physically visiting the concerned Pakistan Mission Abroad. The Project was conceived in May 2021 and formally inaugurated on 18th Nov. 2021. Project was initially rolled out in missions of UK and US. The global roll out is underway.

Succession Certificate
Local Project
In January 2021, NADRA launched a quick five-step process, which facilitates the applicants to receive Letters of Administration or Succession Certificates, as the case may be, without going to the Courts. This has led to reduction of workload on judiciary by 30%. The Project is operational in ICT, Punjab, Sindh and KP at District level sites. Succession Certificate project is currently rolled out across Islamabad, Sindh, Punjab and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. More regions will be added soon to the program. Please visit the official website of this project for more information.

Prime Minister National Health Program
Local Project
Prime Minister’s National Health Program (PMNHP) is a milestone towards social welfare reforms; ensuring that the identified under-privileged citizens across the country get access to their entitled medical health care in a swift and dignified manner without any financial obligations. It ensures the health of underprivileged citizens of the country and is destined to target all falling under the poverty line as per World Health Organization’s standards. Eligibility of treatment is on inpatient basis; including secondary care and priority treatments. Details of the same can be viewed on the PMNHP website.

Watan Card
Local Project
Watan Cards were launched in 2010 to help the victims of floods in Pakistan. NADRA played a vital role in supporting the Government of Pakistan to provide the financial assistance to the flood affected population of Pakistan on a short notice of 72 hours. The 14 Million affected were given relief for fast recovery and their return to normal and comfortable lifestyle. The affected areas were identified; biometrics taken and verified to ensure transparency and maintain records. Watan card provides the beneficiaries to manage their life style with the grant provided and support family members. This project ensured rehabilitation after natural calamities. It has also developed a web based case management system to facilitate appeals and complains of the applicants.

Temporary Displaced Person (FATA)
Local Project
In wake of the militancy hit areas of Pakistan due to the operations in tribal areas to eradicate terrorism, a lot of families have been displaced from their home domain. FATA Disaster Management Authority with support of NADRA has verified citizens for the emergency recovery support. The financial aid is being given to all these deserving persons.
